Evalution

Submissions are evaluated by computing TPR (true positive rate) at a specific FPR(false positive rate). The metric is described as follows:

TPR = TP(true positives) / (TP(true positives) + FN(false negatives))

FPR = FP(false positives) / (FP(false positives) + TN(true negatives))

The final TPR is computed at FPR=1e-5. The participants will be ranked on this final metric.
